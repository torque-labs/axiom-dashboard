## Key Findings Summary

1. **Hold Time is the Primary Filter**
   - 15min reduces total sniper PNL by 88%
   - 20min provides marginal additional reduction (1%)
   - Coordinated bot wallets (5CWn9gFt, JCLqyRAz) are at -99% with just 15min

2. **Volume Threshold ($2k vs $3k) Has No Impact**
   - All scenarios produce identical results for $2k and $3k
   - Snipers and legitimate traders both trade high-volume tokens

3. **Unique Traders Threshold Effects**
   - 10→20: Removes CA4keXLt from top rankings
   - 20→30: No additional changes
   - CA4keXLt may be gaming low-popularity tokens

4. **Recommended Configuration**
   - **Primary:** 15min / $2,000 / 20 Unique Traders
   - Balances sniper reduction with legitimate trader protection
   - Removes potential low-trader token gaming
